— On the curator

I don’t sell cars.
I find them homes.

For eleven years I have moved between Tokyo, Modena, Stuttgart and the Côte d’Azur, hunting the chassis numbers other dealers can’t reach. My clients are pilots, founders, surgeons — quiet people with loud garages.

Every car I touch is inspected, documented, and delivered with a hand-written file. No clocked odometers. No grey imports. No surprises.
